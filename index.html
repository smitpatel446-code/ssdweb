About Us

Igniting Spirits Since 1999

Where Passion Meets Craft

Welcome to Silver Star Distillery, where passion meets craftsmanship in the world of premium spirits. Established in 1999, our journey began with a singular vision — to create exceptional liquors that transcend the ordinary and embody the essence of sophistication.


Founded by Kunal G. Tandel, a visionary entrepreneur with a profound love for the art of distillation. What started as a humble venture has evolved into a distinguished name in the industry, celebrated for its unwavering commitment to excellence.

A Legacy Continued with Silver Star Distillery

The Heritage of Daman

The heritage of distillation in Daman began with the historic Government Distillery, known for its traditional techniques and commitment to quality rum production.

Carrying this legacy forward, Silver Star Distillery blends decades of experience with modern production standards to craft spirits that reflect both tradition and innovation.

One of the oldest and most recognized products from this heritage is Bhenslore White Rum, known for its smooth character and balanced flavor.

The Story

The Secret,
Unveiled

John Martin Secret Malt is a whisky shaped by patience, precision, and quiet confidence — crafted for those who appreciate character beyond the obvious.

Expertly blended using carefully selected malts, this expression delivers a balanced and smooth profile, offering a gentle, cushioned mouthfeel and a long, composed finish.

Every element is designed to harmonize, ensuring a refined drinking experience from first pour to final note.

The Malt & The Maturation

The soul of John Martin Secret Malt lies in its malts. Matured in oak barrels under controlled conditions, the spirit slowly absorbs the essence of wood, time, and craftsmanship. As the barrels breathe, they impart warmth, depth, and complexity — elevating the whisky into a layered and expressive blend.

The Tasting Experience

Aromatic Signature

Aromatic spices intertwine with gentle sweetness and woody undertones, setting the stage for an elegant tasting journey.

On The Palate

Depth & Character

Rich yet smooth, the palate unfolds with a mouthful texture and refined intensity. Expect woody depth, zesty spice, and nut-like savoury notes, culminating in a warm, satisfying finish that lingers effortlessly.

The Finish

Long & Composed

A long composed finish that whispers of oak, spice, and warmth. The kind of finish that rewards patience — every sip revealing a new dimension of this masterfully crafted blend.

Gastronomy

Serving & Pairings

John Martin Secret Malt pairs exceptionally well with medium to spicy cuisine, enhancing both the whisky and the dish.

  • Tandoori selections & kebabs — the smoky char mirrors the whisky's woody depth
  • Fresh seafood — salt and brine contrast beautifully with sweet spice notes
  • Robust Indian flavours — masalas and aromatic gravies amplify the blend's complexity
  • Dark chocolate & aged cheese — for a contemplative, slow-sipping occasion

Recommended Serves

  • Neat — Experience the full complexity at room temperature
  • On the Rocks — A single large ice cube opens the aroma gently
  • With a Splash — A few drops of still water to unlock hidden notes
  • Classic Highball — Over ice with premium soda for easy evenings
